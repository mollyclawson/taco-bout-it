Our Story

Our owners, Maria and Joe Rodriguez, started Taco 'Bout It back in spring of 1987- just seven months after they tied the knot. After graduating high school together, Maria and Joe simply knew that it was their fate to make authentic, handmade Mexican cuisine for the locals of the greater Boston area, especially for the hungry college kids of Somerville, Cambridge, and Boston. At just 21, Maria and Joe bought their first used truck and started making their homemade-quality tacos. In 1995, they purchased a brick-and-mortar unit to start a dine-in and delivery service in Medford.

The Owners

Maria

Maria Rodriguez is the business-savvy brains behind Taco 'Bout It; she's been handling the finances of TBI since its beginnings. Mother to three teenage sons and loyal wife to Joe, she is "the boss" in every sense of the word.

Joe

Joe Rodriguez is a chef at heart. When he first started TBI with his wife Maria, he wasn't interested in making a large proft- but was really focued on making high-quality, authentic, handmade tacos for the locals. He's constantly coming up with new, creative recipes.
